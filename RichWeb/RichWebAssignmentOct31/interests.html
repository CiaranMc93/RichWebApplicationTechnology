Ciaran has a great affinity with GAA. He enjoys playing it and supporting his local team, O'Dempseys, and his county team of Laois. He has won various titles in his time playing GAA such as two Lenister medals and multiple county championship and league medals. He supports his team with pride and is developing an application that will connect the GAA community better than ever before. The application will allow users to view scores of matches in real time. It will be easy to view scores even if you are in a different country and it will engage the interst of fans from around the world. Creating this app was of great fun to Ciaran as he wanted to create a way people can enjoy GAA more and more.
